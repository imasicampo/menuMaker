Who are we?

If you've recently noticed a big crowd gathering on the corner of 30th and Beech Street in South Park it's probably because of Kindred. Our neighborhood restaurant and bar just celebrated our first year and we hope there are many more to come!

Not only do we serve tasty food and drinks, our restaurant is breathtaking. A 35-foot marble bar dominates most of the room, while a four-eyed wolf head sculpture with snakes for horns hangs along the wall.

Over the top? Maybe. Awesome as heck. You bet your butt.
